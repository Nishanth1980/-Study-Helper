Algebra

Quadratic Equations

ax² + bx + c = 0
x = [-b ± √(b²-4ac)]/2a
Example:

Solve x² - 5x + 6 = 0

Solution: x = 2 or x = 3

Linear Equations

y = mx + b
Example:

Find slope of 2y = 4x + 6

Solution: m = 2

Polynomials

(a+b)² = a² + 2ab + b²
(a-b)² = a² - 2ab + b²
Example:

(x+3)² = x² + 6x + 9

Geometry

Pythagorean Theorem

a² + b² = c²
Example:

Right triangle: 3, 4, ?

Solution: Hypotenuse = 5

Circle Formulas

Area = πr²
Circumference = 2πr
Example:

Radius = 7 units

Area: ≈ 153.94 units²

Trigonometry

sinθ = opp/hyp
cosθ = adj/hyp
tanθ = opp/adj
Example:

Right triangle: opp=3, adj=4

tanθ: 0.75

Calculus

Derivatives

d/dx(xⁿ) = nxⁿ⁻¹
Example:

f(x) = 3x² + 2x

f'(x): 6x + 2

Integration

∫xⁿ dx = xⁿ⁺¹/(n+1) + C
Example:

∫(2x + 3) dx

Solution: x² + 3x + C

Limits

lim(x→a) f(x) = L
Example:

lim(x→2) (x²-4)/(x-2) = 4

Math Study Tips

Practice Daily

Consistent practice builds intuition and problem-solving skills. Even 30 minutes daily makes a difference.

Try: Solve 5 problems every morning

Understand Concepts

Focus on why formulas work rather than just memorizing them. Draw diagrams to visualize relationships.

Example: Derive quadratic formula yourself

Teach Others

Explaining concepts to friends helps solidify your understanding and reveals gaps in knowledge.

Try: Form a study group
